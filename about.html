Dr. Debjani Majumdar

Her Story

With over three decades under her belt in horticulture, landscaping, events, wedding planning, flower decorations and interior design, Dr. Debjani Majumdar has created luxury experiences across the country. Her love for nature and passion for design has been showcased across world-class hotels, royal weddings, and prominent events.
Dr. Majumdar holds a Ph.D. in Horticulture and started her career working in the hotel industry with groups like Taj, Oberoi and so on, handling different verticals ranging from landscaping to décor.
With a keen eye for detail and a penchant for creating dynamic luxury designs, she has also planned and produced events like the receiving of the Queen’s baton ceremony for the 2010 Commonwealth Games at the Wagah border, the VIP Circuit for the Grand Prix Formula 1 , the Centenary celebration for Castrol, Narendra Modi and South Korean President inaugurating Samsung Electronics Co. Ltd's mobile phone factory,the largest in the world and many others.
She designed, developed and maintained campuses like the American Embassy School, Tata Apartments, Crowne Plaza hotels, etc in New Delhi, and also standardised the décor for opening hotels by Marriott, Crowne Plaza, the Leela Palace, Oberoi hotels, ITC hotels, Vana Retreat, Hilton , amongst others. Dr. Majumdar has also created world-class displays across the country for hotel openings, launches, and other corporate events.
One of the leading horticulturists and designer in India, she is commissioned by brands like Louis Vuitton, Christin Dior, Salvatore Ferragamo, Roberto Cavalli, Versace, Burberry, Cartier, Massimo Dutti and many more for her artful creations that match those of her international counterparts.
A one stop shop, Dr. Majumdar has designed, planned and produced grand destination weddings at the City Palace in Jaipur, Jagmandir in Udaipur, Beach wedding in Mahabalipuram and various others, and also worked with different organisations like the Dalai Lama Foundation, Vana Foundation, Show Time Events, Wizcrafts, Seventy EMG and numerous others.
Dr. Majumdar has been a faculty for horticulture and decor at various institutions like the Oberoi School and Marriott and has been featured in Hotelier magazine for her spectacular Christmas installations at the Grand Hyatt in Mumbai. She was recognised by Cosmopolitan as a woman working in a profession out of the ordinary.
Her passion for art and culture intertwined with her experience in the hospitality industry inspired her to create a niche homestay experience in the old town of Kolkata. Her homestay is listed on Airbnb as Hoteliers Abode in Kolkata.
